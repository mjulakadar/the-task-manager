Services and Dependency Injection

1. Understanding Services

Services in Angular are singleton objects that are instantiated only once during the lifetime of an application. They are used to share data, logic, and functions across various parts of an application. Services help to encapsulate business logic, data retrieval, and other functionalities which can be reused and tested independently.

2. Dependency Injection (DI)

Dependency Injection (DI) is a design pattern used to implement IoC (Inversion of Control), allowing Angular to create dependencies and inject them into components or services where they are needed. This mechanism improves code modularity, reusability, and testability.
