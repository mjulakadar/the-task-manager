Angular Forms

1. Angular Froms

In Angular, there are two primary approaches to handling forms: Template-Driven Forms and Reactive Forms. Both approaches are used to capture and validate user input, but they have different philosophies and use cases.

2. Template-Driven Forms

Template-driven forms are simpler and suitable for simple forms. They rely heavily on Angular's directives and the template itself to handle the form.
